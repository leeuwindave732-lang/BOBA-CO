Our Story

Founded in 2020, BOBA CO. has been dedicated to serving exceptional milk tea to our customers.

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and traditional brewing methods to deliver an unparalleled milk tea experience. Our team is passionate about milk tea and committed to excellence in every cup.
